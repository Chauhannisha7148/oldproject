Delivery Model

Dedicated team model

If you are looking to develop a web application for your company, but don't have enough time to regularly communicate with each team member, this model is for you. You can hire an entire team of dedicated developers, UI/UX designers & testers, special consultants, etc. for your project who will work as your own. In addition, a project manager will be assigned who will oversee the entire team and keep you updated on the project's progress.

This model gives you the freedom to choose the team you feel most comfortable with and pay only for the resources you use or hire.

Offsite delivery model

We excel in providing the right solution through an Offsite delivery model that takes care of all the problems from the vicinity. Our team of qualified IT professionals assigned to your project provides constant support to your local needs. Our team creates a local presence and ensures faster communication with clarity.

Onsite delivery model

In this model, we carry out the project execution on your business location. With our skilled team on your site, we ensure Onsite delivery, which takes care of all the complexities required to complete the job. Our skilled developers, quality analysts, testers, and deployment specialists ensure that your project is completed on time.

Offshore delivery model

For project development, this model allows you to have a virtual extension of your own in-house team. The OffShore Delivery module handles all of the minute details and challenges that add value to our OffShore Clients. Our offshore team communicates with the client at all stages of the project. The entire team reports directly to you so that you have unrestricted access.

Global delivery model

This model enables you to take advantage of utilizing people and other resources from around the world, to deliver high-quality solutions under tight deadlines, with a focus on quality and risk reduction. With our Global Delivery Model, we help organizations achieve globalization. Our technical expertise enables large-scale implementation of any process, resulting in global benefits.
